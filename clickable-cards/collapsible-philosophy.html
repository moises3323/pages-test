Food Philosophy

  • We honor our role in the food supply chain by maintaining high standards for the products on our shelves, the ingredients in our kitchens, and the practices on our farms.

  • We focus on practices that protect nutritional density and maximize bioavailability, so you get the most out of every bite.

  • We vet every product and ingredient before bringing it into our stores or kitchens, because food should be made of food.

  • We prioritize sourcing from farmers and producers who honor heritage practices and uphold high ethical standards for people, animals, and the environment.
